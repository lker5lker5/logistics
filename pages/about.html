About DigiSoft

DigiSoft Intelligent Logistics is a website developed for assisting logistics management in small to medium scale courier companies.

The prime idea for development of this website is to enable informed decision making for logistics analysts, based on the historic and current data. This information will ultimately provide an effective plan for transportation of vehicles for timely delivery from point of origin to their destination; taking into consideration the analysis of traffic, safety and weather.

About The System

DigiSoft Intelligent Logistics is a part of the project developed by students of Monash University. We are final year Masters students from Faculty of Information Technology.

This website exists because VicRoads make their data about crashes and traffic volume openly available to all users. The source of data for this website is VicRoads open data.

It is difficult to get relevant data related to accidents and historic traffic on a regular basis. We have tried to provide a system for analysts to assist them to find the safest and most efficient route within the state of Victoria based on the data available to us. DigiSoft Intelligent Logistics allows you to view the historical accidents data from the years 2010 upto 2015.

Technologies Used

Google Maps API is used to show the routes on the map. We chose Google Maps as The Google Maps APIs give developers several ways of embedding Google Maps into web pages, and allows for either simple use or extensive customization.

For depicting the visual representations like line charts and bar graphs, chart.js has been used. ChartJS provides beautiful flat designs for charts. It uses HTML5 canvas element for rendering. These charts are responsive by default. They work well in mobiles and tablets, which was an added advantage as this is a responsive website.
